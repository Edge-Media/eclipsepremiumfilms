Our products

Solar film

Solar films use sputtered film and nano-ceramic film with heat-repellent and heat-absorbing technology to reduce heat and block UV rays. They’re ideal for homes, offices, and vehicles to improve comfort, protect interiors, and save energy.

Privacy film

Privacy films are designed to block visibility, enhance security, reduce glare, and control UV exposure. Available in various types—such as one-way mirror films, frosted or etched films, and tinted films—they are commonly used in homes, offices, medical facilities, and vehicles to protect occupants’ privacy and prevent outsiders from seeing inside.

Safety film

Safety films are designed to hold shattered glass together, preventing injuries from sharp edges and flying debris. They’re often used in homes, offices, and vehicles to enhance security, reduce the risk of injury from accidents or break-ins, and provide protection against natural disasters like storms or earthquakes.

Decorative film

Decorative films are designed to enhance interior aesthetics while providing added privacy. Available in frosted, etched, and patterned styles, they’re commonly used in homes, offices, and commercial spaces to transform glass surfaces without blocking natural light.
